Safari Extensions

Google Reader Max OS X Snow Leopard + Delicious

Originally ported from Google Reader: Mac OS X Snow Leopard and Google Reader: Max OS X Snow Leopard + Delicious. and now available as a safari extension!

I also fixed one or two parts that were no longer working since a few reader updates have been released since the last updates to the userscripts.

(14 Jun, 2010: Added to GitHub)
